Welcome to McDan White Water Rafting

Our Mission

McDan White Water Rafting has been providing thrilling and safe river adventures since 2010. Our team of certified guides has years of experience navigating rivers of all levels, ensuring every guest enjoys an unforgettable experience. We focus on safety, fun, and environmental stewardship, giving you not just an adventure, but a connection to nature. From family-friendly trips to adrenaline-pumping rapids, we offer experiences tailored to every skill level.. Safety first, adventure always!

History

McDan White Water Rafting began in 2010 with a simple idea — to share the thrill of river rafting and the beauty of nature with everyone. What started as a small, family-run operation with just one raft has grown into a trusted adventure company known for safety, professionalism, and unforgettable outdoor experiences. Over the years, we’ve expanded our team, upgraded our equipment, and explored new rivers, all while staying true to our roots — creating lasting memories and deep connections with nature. Our history reflects our passion for adventure and our commitment to providing every guest with an exceptional experience on the water.
